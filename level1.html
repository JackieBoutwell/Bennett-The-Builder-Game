Bennett the Builder Game

Welcome to "Bennett the Builder"! In this game, you'll embark on a thrilling adventure to locate Bennett's scattered tools across various construction sites. Get ready to sharpen your memory and observational skills as you navigate through the challenges. Let's dive into the instructions:

The main goal of the game is to find and collect all your scattered tools within the specified time limit. Each level presents a new set of tools to locate, and as you progress, the difficulty increases.

1. **Starting a Level:**
- Select a level to begin. Initially, only the first level will be available. As you successfully complete levels, you'll unlock more challenging ones.
2. **Tool List:**
- Before starting the level, you'll be presented with a list of tools you need to find. Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the names and shapes of these tools.
3. **Construction Site Exploration:**
- Once the level begins, you'll find yourself looking at cards.Use the mouse and click the cards to help you find the missing tools.
4. **Interaction:** - To pick up a tool, move close to it and click on it with your mouse. The tool will then be added to your inventory.
6. **Completing a Level:** - Once you've collected all the required tools within the time limit, you'll proceed to the next level. Celebrate your success and get ready for more challenging tasks ahead!
